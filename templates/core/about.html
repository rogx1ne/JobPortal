About

Career Aggregator is a job discovery platform that pulls job listings from the public Remotive Jobs API. It helps you search by keyword and refine results with filters, then redirects you to the original posting to apply.

  • No user accounts required
  • Searches are logged in MySQL for analytics
  • Cached jobs are stored for admin visibility

Disclaimer: Job availability and details are provided by third parties and may change at any time.
